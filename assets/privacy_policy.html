Privacy Policy

CrystalPopperz

✅ No Data Collection 📴 Fully Offline 🔓 No Permissions 🚫 No Tracking 📱 On-Device Only

📋 Overview

CrystalPopperz ("the game", "we", "our") is committed to your privacy. This policy explains how we handle — or more accurately, how we don't handle — your information.

🚫 No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. This includes:

  • Name, email address, or account information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any other personal or sensitive information

📱 Local Storage Only

Game progress (levels completed, stars earned, high scores) and settings (music on/off) are stored only on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is never uploaded to any server
  • Can be cleared by uninstalling the app

🔓 No Permissions Required

CrystalPopperz requests zero Android permissions. We do not access:

  • Camera or microphone
  • Contacts or phone state
  • Location (GPS or network)
  • Storage or files
  • Internet or network access

📡 No Internet Access

The game operates completely offline. No network requests are ever made. There are no servers, no cloud saves, and no online features.

🧩 No Third-Party SDKs

We do not integrate any third-party:

  • Analytics platforms (e.g. Firebase, Mixpanel)
  • Advertising networks (e.g. AdMob, Meta Audience)
  • Crash reporting tools
  • Social media SDKs

👶 Children's Privacy

CrystalPopperz is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. Because we collect no data whatsoever, there are no privacy concerns for any age group. The game fully complies with COPPA, GDPR-K, and similar regulations.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this Privacy Policy, the updated version will be included within the app. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
